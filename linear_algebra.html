matrix product transpose trace symmetric block matrix Schur complement
深度學習中的梯度計算到處都是矩陣乘法。Attention mechanism 的 Q、K、V 也是矩陣運算。 Gradient computation is all matrix ops. Q/K/V in attention are matrix operations.

Determinants ｜行列式
Properties, geometric meaning, cofactor expansion
determinant volume scaling singular cofactor Cramer's rule
行列式 = 0 表示矩陣是奇異的（不可逆）。這在電網分析中標誌著臨界狀態。 det=0 means singular/non-invertible — signals critical states in grid analysis.

Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors ｜特徵值與特徵向量
Characteristic polynomial, diagonalization, complex eigenvalues

PCA 就是找 covariance matrix 的 eigenvalues。電網模式分析（Modal analysis）也靠 eigenvalues 判斷穩定性。 PCA = eigenvalues of covariance matrix. Grid modal analysis uses eigenvalues for stability.
Ch 7
Orthogonality ｜正交性
Inner products, orthogonal bases, projections, Gram-Schmidt
inner product orthogonal projection Gram-Schmidt orthonormal basis least squares
最小平方法迴歸就是找最佳投影。信號處理、壓縮、迴歸分析的基礎。 Least squares = best projection. Foundation of signal processing, compression, regression.
Ch 8
Matrix Decompositions ｜矩陣分解
SVD · PCA · LU · QR · Cholesky · Eigendecomposition
SVD PCA LU decomposition QR decomposition Cholesky Jordan form
現代 ML 的核心。SVD 用於推薦系統和降維。PCA = SVD on covariance。Deep learning 的權重矩陣也依賴這些分解。 Heart of modern ML. SVD for recommender systems & dimensionality reduction. Deep learning weights rely on these.

📖 Core Vocabulary | 核心詞彙

Vector Space
向量空間
A collection of vectors that can be added together and multiplied by scalars. The arena where all linear algebra happens.
Linear Transformation
線性變換
A mapping between vector spaces that preserves vector addition and scalar multiplication. Every linear transformation can be represented by a matrix.
Eigenvalue / Eigenvector
特徵值 / 特徵向量
Ax = λx. The eigenvector is the direction that doesn't change under transformation A; eigenvalue λ is how much it stretches.
Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)
奇異值分解
A = UΣV^T. The Swiss Army knife of matrix decompositions. Exists for all matrices. Used in PCA, recommendation systems, dimensionality reduction.
Orthogonal
正交
Two vectors are orthogonal when their dot product = 0. The foundation of projections, least squares, and Fourier analysis.
Rank
秩
The number of linearly independent rows (or columns) of a matrix. Rank tells you the "true dimensionality" of your data.
Determinant
行列式
det(A) = volume scaling factor of transformation A. det=0 → singular (non-invertible) → information loss.
Basis
基底
A set of linearly independent vectors that spans the entire vector space. You can represent any vector uniquely as a linear combination of basis vectors.
Projection
投影
Mapping a vector onto a subspace. Least squares regression = finding the best projection of data onto a model subspace.
Norm
範數
A measure of vector "length" or "magnitude". L2 norm (Euclidean distance) is most common. L1 norm for sparsity.
Condition Number
條件數
The ratio of largest to smallest singular value. High condition number → numerical instability → hard to solve accurately.
Kernel / Null Space
核空間 / 零空間
The set of vectors that map to zero under a transformation. In ML: tells you what information is destroyed by a linear map.

🎯 Why This Matters for Your Research | 為什麼這對你的研究重要

📡 Smart Grid Anomaly Detection

Time-series sensor data → matrix (window of readings) → PCA/SVD for dimensionality reduction → detect anomalies via reconstruction error. Understanding eigenvalues = understanding why this works.

🔬 Digital Twin Modeling

State estimation in power systems = solving overdetermined linear systems. Jacobian matrix in Newton-Raphson = linearization of nonlinear power flow. LA is the engine.

🧠 Deep Learning

Every neural network is linear algebra under the hood. Backpropagation is the chain rule for matrix derivatives. Understanding LA → intuition for why networks work and why they fail.
